My name is Ryan Timmons, and I have a talent for developing mobile and web applications.

I discovered my passion and knack for software engineering while writing macros and scripts in order to automate daily tasks for my accounting internship. I observed software’s power to solve everyday problems and I experienced the satisfaction of crafting those solutions. Shortly after graduating from a top business program at the University of Texas, I pursued a career in software development and architecture. My drive and energy has exposed me to many languages, tools, frameworks, and development methodologies. Every day, I’m thrilled to create applications that change the face of business and user interaction.

RecordStore

This was my first foray into end-to-end development and deployment of an application built on an MVC framework. This application allowed users to manage a collection of their records and see various information about the albums in their choice of layout. The application was built in Ruby on Rails, and used a Postgres database with jQuery on the front-end. This application is extremely primitive by my current standards, but it kicked off an avalanche of progress afterward.
